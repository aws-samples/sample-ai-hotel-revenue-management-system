Help & Documentation

Getting Started
Welcome to the Hotel Revenue Optimization System

This system helps you optimize your hotel's revenue by providing data-driven recommendations based on market analysis, demand forecasting, and pricing strategies.

Two Ways to Use the System
Natural Language

Ask questions or request recommendations in plain English. For example:

  • "Optimize pricing for my 4-star hotel in Miami during summer"
  • "Forecast demand for next quarter for my business hotel"
  • "Analyze competitor pricing for luxury hotels in my area"
Try Natural Language
Structured Form

Fill out a form with specific details about your hotel and requirements:

  • Hotel type, location, and star rating
  • Season and current occupancy rate
  • Optimization goals and competitor analysis
Try Structured Form
Tips for Best Results
For Natural Language Queries
  • Be specific about your hotel type, location, and time period
  • Mention your current challenges or goals
  • Include current occupancy rates or pricing if available
  • Specify if you want competitor analysis
For Structured Form
  • Fill out all fields for the most accurate recommendations
  • Be specific with your location (city, neighborhood)
  • Select the most appropriate optimization goal
  • Check the competitor analysis box for market positioning
Understanding Results

The system provides comprehensive recommendations that typically include:

  • Executive Summary - A high-level overview of the recommendations
  • Market Analysis - Insights into current market conditions
  • Pricing Recommendations - Specific pricing strategies for different room types and periods
  • Distribution Strategy - Recommendations for channel management
  • Implementation Plan - Step-by-step guide to implement the recommendations
  • Expected Outcomes - Projected results from implementing the recommendations
Viewing History

All your queries and their results are saved in the History page for future reference.
